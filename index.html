About us

Welcome to Coffee Dose – your daily fix of warmth, flavor, and comfort.
At Coffee Dose, we believe a good cup of coffee has the power to brighten your day. Whether you're here for your morning boost, an afternoon catch-up, or a cozy corner to unwind, we’ve crafted the perfect space for you.
Our beans are carefully sourced, our brews are passionately made, and every cup is served with a smile. From rich espressos to creamy lattes, refreshing iced blends to delightful desserts, Coffee Dose is more than just a coffee shop – it’s a community hub where stories are shared and moments are made.
Come for the coffee, stay for the vibe. ☕✨
